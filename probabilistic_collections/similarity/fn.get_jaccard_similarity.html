pub fn get_jaccard_similarity<T, U>(iter_1: T, iter_2: T) -> f64 where
    T: Iterator<Item = U>,
    U: Hash + Eq, 

Computes the Jaccard Similarity between two iterators. The Jaccard Similarity is the quotient between the intersection and the union.

Examples

use probabilistic_collections::similarity::{get_jaccard_similarity, ShingleIterator};

assert_eq!(
    get_jaccard_similarity(
        ShingleIterator::new(2, "the cat sat on a mat".split(' ').collect()),
        ShingleIterator::new(2, "the cat sat on the mat".split(' ').collect()),
    ),
    3.0 / 7.0,
);
